💡 How to Bet on Polymarket

Complete guide to understanding and placing bets on prediction markets

🎯 What is Polymarket?

Polymarket is a decentralized prediction market where you can bet on real-world events. Unlike traditional gambling, you're trading shares based on your prediction of future outcomes.

When an event resolves, winning shares pay out $1.00 per share, while losing shares become worthless.

📚 Step-by-Step Guide

1️⃣ Find a Market

Browse markets on the Markets page. Markets cover politics, sports, crypto, entertainment, and more.

💡 Tip: Look for markets with high liquidity ($50K+) for better prices

2️⃣ Understand the Odds

Prices represent the market's probability of an outcome:

  • $0.75 = 75% probability (likely to happen)
  • $0.50 = 50% probability (coin flip)
  • $0.25 = 25% probability (unlikely)

💡 Tip: If you think the probability is higher than the price, BUY. If lower, SELL.

3️⃣ Calculate Your Returns

Example: Buy "Yes"

  • Buy 100 shares at $0.70 = Cost: $70
  • If YES wins: Get $100 (100 shares × $1.00)
  • Profit: $30 (43% return)

Example: Buy "No"

  • Buy 100 shares at $0.30 = Cost: $30
  • If NO wins: Get $100 (100 shares × $1.00)
  • Profit: $70 (233% return)

💡 Tip: Higher risk (lower probability) = Higher potential returns

4️⃣ Place Your Trade

Connect your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.) and fund it with USDC on Polygon network.

Choose your outcome (YES/NO), enter your amount, and confirm the trade.

5️⃣ Monitor & Exit

You can sell your shares anytime before the market resolves. Prices change based on new information.

⚠️ Risk Management

  • Never bet more than you can afford to lose
  • Diversify across multiple markets instead of going all-in on one
  • Set limits - decide your max position size in advance
  • Do your research - understand the event you're betting on
  • Watch liquidity - low liquidity markets can be harder to exit
  • Consider timing - prices are most volatile when news breaks
