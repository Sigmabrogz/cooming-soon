Automatically follow and copy trades from successful traders. Learn by watching the pros!

💡 How Copy Trading Works

1️⃣ Choose a Trader to Follow

Browse our Top Traders leaderboard and select traders based on:

  • Win Rate: Higher is better (60%+ is excellent)
  • Total Volume: Shows experience and activity level
  • ROI: Measures profitability over time
  • Risk Score: Lower scores indicate more consistent performance

2️⃣ Configure Copy Settings

Customize how you want to copy their trades:

  • Copy Percentage: Copy 10% of their trade size (adjustable)
  • Max Position Size: Set maximum $ per position ($100 default)
  • Max Total Exposure: Limit total capital deployed ($1000 default)
  • Market Filters: Choose which markets to copy (or exclude)
  • Min Trade Size: Only copy trades above certain size
  • Auto Exit: Automatically copy their exit trades too

3️⃣ Monitor Performance

Track your copy trading performance in real-time. See which trades were copied, your P&L, and compare your results to the original trader.

⚠️ Important Considerations

Copy trading is not a guarantee: Past performance doesn't guarantee future results. Even the best traders have losing streaks.

Understand the risks: You're still responsible for your trades. Make sure you understand what you're copying.

Set appropriate limits: Never risk more than you can afford to lose. Use the maximum exposure settings wisely.

Monitor regularly: Check your copy trading performance regularly and adjust or stop following if needed.

🎓 Example Scenario

Trader "CryptoWhale" makes a trade:

  • Buys 1,000 shares of "Bitcoin to $100K by 2025" at $0.65
  • Total cost: $650

Your copy trading settings:

  • Copy percentage: 10%
  • Max position size: $100

What happens automatically:

  • System calculates: 10% of 1,000 shares = 100 shares
  • Cost would be: $65
  • Since $65 < $100 max, trade is copied
  • You buy 100 shares at $0.65
  • If Bitcoin hits $100K, your 100 shares pay $100
  • Your profit: $35 (54% return)
